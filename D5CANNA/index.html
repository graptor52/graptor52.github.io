County of San Luis Obispo · District 5 Cannabis Map

Data Limitations & Disclaimer

The information presented on this map is approximate and subject to known limitations. Projects that span multiple APNs may be represented by a single parcel record due to data constraints. Cannabis code enforcement cases have not been included. Records were identified using keyword searches of available application data; as a result, some records may not have been captured due to variations in terminology, typographical errors in project descriptions, or incomplete source data. This data set is considered substantially complete, but it should not be relied upon as a comprehensive record of projects. Addresses are approximate and based on available data.
